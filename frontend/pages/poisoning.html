Poisoning

First aid for toxic exposure
Medical Emergency Poisoning requires immediate professional action. Identify the substance if safe to do so.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • 1
    Call emergency services (108 / 112) immediately.
  • 2
    If inhaled, move the person to fresh air.
  • 3
    If on skin or eyes, flush with water for 15-20 minutes.
  • 4
    Check the label/bottle of the poison if possible.
  • 5
    Do not induce vomiting unless told by a doctor.

What NOT To Do

  • Do not induce vomiting.
  • Do not give anything by mouth to an unconscious person.
  • Do not wait for symptoms to appear before calling help.
