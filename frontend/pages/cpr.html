CPR

Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation
Medical Emergency If the person is unconscious and not breathing normally, call emergency services immediately and start CPR.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • 1
    Check responsiveness. Tap and shout.
  • 2
    Call 108 or 112 immediately.
  • 3
    Start chest compressions in the center of the chest.
  • 4
    Give rescue breaths if trained and comfortable.
  • 5
    Continue until help arrives or the person starts breathing.

What NOT To Do

  • Do not stop compressions unnecessarily.
  • Do not wait for an ambulance before starting CPR.
  • Do not leave the victim alone.
