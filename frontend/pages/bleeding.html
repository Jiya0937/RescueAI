Bleeding

Stop severe external blood loss
Medical Emergency Severe bleeding can be life-threatening. Stop the bleeding as quickly as possible.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • 1
    Apply direct pressure on the wound.
  • 2
    Use a clean cloth or bandage.
  • 3
    Elevate the injured area if possible.
  • 4
    Apply more pressure if bleeding continues.
  • 5
    Seek medical help immediately.

What NOT To Do

  • Do not remove blood-soaked cloth.
  • Do not wash the wound.
  • Do not leave the wound uncovered.
